Our Story

Built with sustainability at its core.

50% of the world's cinnamon originates from Indonesia. Most of it grows in one unique area on the island of Sumatra, called Kerinci. Cassia Co-op is the first cinnamon processing and exporting company to set up right here, at the heart of the plantations.

We created a fair and efficient supply chain while having a sustainable positive impact. No middlemen. No guesswork. Just a direct line from the farmers to your facility.

Our Origin

Kerinci, Sumatra

The world's largest cassia plantation sits in the Kerinci Valley of Jambi Province, Sumatra. Volcanic soil, tropical climate, and generations of farming expertise create the perfect conditions for the finest cinnamon on earth.

"We didn't choose the location. The cinnamon chose it, thousands of years ago. We just set up where it makes sense."

We export directly to Europe, the United States, and markets worldwide. Our proximity to the source means shorter lead times, fresher product, and full traceability from tree to shipment.
