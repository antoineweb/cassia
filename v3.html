About Us

Where exceptional
cinnamon begins

Deep within the volcanic highlands of Sumatra lies the Kerinci Valley — a place where altitude, climate, and centuries of farming tradition converge to produce some of the world's most prized cassia cinnamon.

Cassia Co-op was founded with a singular conviction: the best way to guarantee quality, fairness, and sustainability is to be present at the very origin. We set up our processing facility directly in Kerinci — making us the first operator of our kind in the region.

Our cooperative model creates a direct, transparent link between over 1,000 local farming families and international buyers. No unnecessary intermediaries. Full traceability. A supply chain built on mutual trust.

Products

It all starts
with the rainforest

The extraordinary quality of Kerinci cinnamon begins long before the harvest. Volcanic soils enriched by rainforest ecosystems, precise altitude, and a microclimate unique to this corner of Sumatra create the ideal conditions for cassia of exceptional character.

Our partner farmers cultivate their plots within and around Kerinci's protected forest areas, following agroforestry practices that preserve biodiversity while producing cinnamon of the highest grade.

Sustainability

Built for
generations

Our approach to sustainability isn't a side programme — it is the operating model. From the seedlings in the ground to the certifications on our export documents, every decision reflects our commitment to the Kerinci ecosystem and the communities who depend on it.

👨‍🌾

Meet our Farmers

We partner directly with over 1,000 farming families in Kerinci. Long-term relationships, fair prices, and shared knowledge — no anonymous middlemen in our chain.

🔍

Traceability

Every batch is traceable to the individual farmer who harvested it. QR-coded documentation accompanies each shipment, giving buyers complete visibility from field to facility.

🧺

Collecting

We manage our own collection network directly in Kerinci villages, cutting transit time and ensuring bark freshness. Controlled collection points with consistent grading on-site.

🌱

Seedlings Programme

Our cooperative distributes certified cassia seedlings to partner farmers — maintaining genetic quality, supporting replanting cycles, and protecting the long-term productivity of Kerinci's plantations.

🌍

Community Impact

Through our CSR programme we invest in local infrastructure, education initiatives, and health access — because a thriving cooperative depends on a thriving community.

🌲

Ecosystem Protection

We work within the boundaries of Kerinci's protected landscapes, applying agroforestry principles that enhance rather than deplete biodiversity across the valley.

Quality

Quality that starts
in the field

Our quality system is not limited to the factory floor. It begins the moment a seedling is planted and continues through every step of the supply chain — with independent verification at each stage.

01
🌾

Starts in the Field

Our field agronomists work alongside farmers to ensure correct harvesting techniques, bark thickness standards, and moisture management at source — before the product ever reaches our facility.

02
🔬

Own Laboratory

Our on-site laboratory in Kerinci runs incoming inspections and in-process controls: oil content, moisture levels, microbiology, foreign matter, and organoleptic assessment at every production stage.

03
🏛️

External Testing

All export batches are independently tested by accredited third-party laboratories. Results are shared proactively with buyers as part of our shipment documentation package.

04
💬

Improving by Customer Feedback

Customer feedback is systematically reviewed by our quality team. We operate a closed-loop improvement process — complaints, suggestions and specifications are logged, acted upon, and reported back.

Factory — Since

Processing
at the source

Since 2009, our processing facility has operated directly in Kerinci — the only one of its kind in the region. This proximity to origin is not a convenience; it is the foundation of everything we offer: freshness, speed, and an unbroken chain of custody.

Our facility is FSSC 22000 certified and continuously upgraded based on customer requirements and technology advances in spice processing.

Visit the Factory
☀️

Drying

Controlled solar and mechanical drying processes reduce moisture to precise levels, preserving volatile oils and preventing microbial growth.

⚙️

Crushing

Multi-stage crushing equipment breaks bark into consistent grades. Dust-free environment, calibrated to customer specifications.

🌀

Grinding

Precision grinding lines produce powder of specified particle size. In-line testing ensures oil content and colour standards are maintained throughout.

📏

Production Sticks

Hand and machine-rolled sticks produced in short and long formats to retail and food service specifications. Consistent diameter, tight curl, clean cuts.
