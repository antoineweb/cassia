About Us

Making a difference at the source

We do not claim to be changing the world. What we can confidently say is that we are making a positive and tangible contribution at the very source of the cassia supply chain.

For us, a supply chain is truly sustainable only when it is fair to all stakeholders — from farmers to end users — and pragmatic from a logistical standpoint.

We are the first and only processor-exporter of cassia to establish operations directly in Kerinci, Sumatra, allowing us to work in direct contact with farmers and ensure full traceability from origin to end user.

Our Promises

Supply chain — Fair, Efficient
and Fully Certified

Sustainability is more than a label — it's our daily reality. We welcome you to visit and see for yourself.

🌱

Sustainability First

Every decision we make is evaluated through an environmental lens. From agroforestry practices to biodegradable packaging, we're building a supply chain that protects the Kerinci ecosystem for generations to come.

Rainforest Alliance Certified
🤝

Farmer Fairness

We work directly with over 1,000 local farmers, guaranteeing fair prices and eliminating exploitative middlemen. Our CSR program reinvests into community development, education, and better farming infrastructure.

Direct Partnership
⭐

Uncompromising Quality

Certified to FSSC 22000 and Organic standards, our quality control processes trace every batch from the individual farmer's plot to the shipping container. Consistent grade, every time.

FSSC 22000 · Organic
🔍

Full Traceability

Every shipment comes with complete documentation — from which farmer harvested the bark, to the processing date and quality test results. We believe transparency builds lasting partnerships.

Batch-Level Tracking
🚢

Direct Export

As the only processor based in Kerinci, we export directly to Europe, the United States and beyond. Shorter supply chain means fresher product, better pricing, and a smaller carbon footprint.

No Intermediaries
🌍

Community Impact

We measure success not just in export volumes, but in community wellbeing. Our cooperative model ensures that the prosperity of Cassia Co-op is shared with the farmers and families who make it possible.
