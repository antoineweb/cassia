About Us

Making a difference at the source

For us, a supply chain is truly sustainable only when it is fair to all stakeholders — from farmers to end users — and pragmatic from a logistical standpoint.

We are the first and only processor-exporter of cassia to establish operations directly in Kerinci, Sumatra, allowing us to work in direct contact with farmers and ensure full traceability from origin to end user.

Our Promises

Sustainability

3:1 Replanting Commitment
For every tree harvested, we replant more than three—securing a sustainable future supply

Local Economic Impact
The only industrial cassia processor operating in the region. Our day-to-day operations support the local economy and community through job creation, tax contributions, partnerships with local stakeholders & institutions…

Farmers Fairness & Transparency

Clear Purchasing Standards (SOP)
A structured system defines exactly what is expected—sorting and drying.

Transparent Pricing
No more guesswork or arbitrary negotiations. Both sides know the rules—farmers understand how value is determined. A bit of extra effort on preparation translates into better prices.

No More Grey Areas
Buying cassia bark becomes clear, consistent, and fair for everyone involved.

Full Traceability

From Field to Facility to Customer
Every lot is traced back to the exact field where it was harvested.

Pre-Harvest Field Checks on all certified cassia (RA, UEBT, ORG, FSA). Our ICS team visits farms before harvest to verify conditions on the ground.

Barcode Tracking (UPC System)
Each batch is recorded upon arrival and tracked throughout our facility.
